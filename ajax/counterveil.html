Counterveil

Project Info

My colleagues and I aimed to develop a visually appealing and responsive single-page landing page for Counterveil, a cybersecurity company. Our objective was to create a website that would showcase the company's unique approach to cybersecurity and expertise in the industry.

To ensure a seamless user experience across all devices, we built the website using HTML, CSS, JS, and Bootstrap. Our design approach focused on creating a simple yet elegant layout that would effectively communicate Counterveil's core principles and value proposition to potential customers.
